Extension Methods You Can Learn

   
     
               
         

Dive deep into the art of K-Tip extensions, a highly sought-after method known for creating natural, 360-degree movement. In this module, you will master the delicate process of attaching individual strands using a specialized heating tool to bond keratin tips.

       
     
     
               
         

Discover the world of heat-free extensions with I-Tips and Nano Beads. This training focuses on the meticulous strand-by-strand application using tiny, secure beads for a seamless, natural finish that is both durable and easy to maintain.

       
     
     
               
         

Master the most popular and fastest method in the industry: Tape-In extensions. This course teaches efficiency and precision, covering the flawless alignment of adhesive wefts to create a full, voluminous look and damage-free removal.

       
     
     
      _         
         

Learn the foundational and highly versatile technique of installing Hand-Tied Wefts. This comprehensive training will show you how to build a secure and flat foundation using beads and master correct stitching patterns for a flawless result.
