Extension Methods You Can Learn

Dive deep into the art of K-Tip extensions, a highly sought-after method known for creating natural, 360-degree movement. In this module, you will master the delicate process of attaching individual strands using a specialized heating tool to bond keratin tips. Joanna will guide you through crucial techniques like precise sectioning for clean installs, optimal bond placement to ensure client comfort and invisibility, and the safe removal process that protects hair integrity.

Discover the world of heat-free extensions with I-Tips and Nano Beads. This training focuses on the meticulous strand-by-strand application using tiny, secure beads. You will learn the correct way to thread beads, how to use pliers for secure clamping without causing damage, and the art of strategic placement for a seamless, natural finish that is both durable and easy to maintain.

Master the most popular and fastest method in the industry: Tape-In extensions. This course teaches efficiency and precision, covering the flawless alignment of adhesive wefts to create a full, voluminous look. You will learn professional techniques for a clean application, damage-free removal, and the essential skill of re-taping and reapplying wefts for repeat clients.

Learn the foundational and highly versatile technique of installing Hand-Tied Wefts. This comprehensive training will show you how to build a secure and flat foundation using beads. You will master correct stitching patterns, how to maintain proper tension to prevent damage, and the expert finishing work required to blend the wefts seamlessly for a flawless and comfortable result.
